# Pillars 🏛️

Pillars is a fast-paced, RNG-based survival game built for Roblox. Players spawn on isolated pillars high in the sky and must rely on luck, quick thinking, and strategy to survive. Every 10 seconds, the game hands you a random block or item—use what you get to defend yourself, build to other players, or knock them into the void.

Your goal is simple: Be the last player standing.

## How to Play

### Keyboard & Mouse
Left click: Place block & attack/throw
WASD: Move around
Spacebar: Jump
1,2,3,4,5 or Scrollwheel: Switch items in hotbar (Can be changed in settings)
Q: Delete Item
Shift: Sneak

### Mobile
Tap on screen: Place/destroy
Attack button: Attack/throw
Sneak button: Sneak
Delete button: Delete Item

## 🎮 Gameplay Mechanics

**Isolated Spawns:** Every player starts the match on their own individual pillar.

**The 10-Second Rule:** Every 10 seconds, the RNG system grants each player a random block, weapon, or utility item.

**Adapt and Survive:** You might get a powerful weapon or just a basic building block. You have to adapt your strategy based on the items you are given.

**Last One Standing:** Build bridges, sabotage your opponents, or simply out-survive the chaos. The last player left alive wins the round.

**Full Party System:** There is a full system for players to create & join parties then be queued together in the game.
